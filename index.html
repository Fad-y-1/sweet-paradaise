Our Story.. How it All Began

In the heart of the magical city of Hurghada, specifically nestled within the elegant streets of Paradise Village, the journey of Sweet Paradise began as a passionate dream to spread happiness through exquisite tastes. It wasn't just a regular dessert truck; it was born out of a genuine love for crafting luxurious flavors that take you on a journey of pure indulgence.

We started by serving the most delicious crepe rolls packed with rich chocolate, fluffy pancakes, and masterfully crafted milkshakes. Every single item we create is made with love and precision to suit the refined taste of the compound’s residents and visitors, making our truck the ultimate destination for sweetness and joyful moments in Hurghada.
